About Us

The primary goal of the College of Dentistry is to remain at the forefront of dental medical education and scientific research in various fields of oral and dental health.

Vision

Leadership in patient care, community, community and education workers in the field of oral and maxillofacial health at the level of the Republic.

the message

That the University Dental Hospital employ all its resources to support oral health care by empowering and inspiring the medical staff and employees of the hospital and supporting students, trainees and faculty members of all levels to achieve the desired goals. This is done through: Enhance communication Bridging the gap between requirements and available resources Support diversity and inclusion. Support career progression/development.

values

The University Dental Hospital is committed to promoting the following values: Moral mercy Respect confidentiality dignity Leadership
